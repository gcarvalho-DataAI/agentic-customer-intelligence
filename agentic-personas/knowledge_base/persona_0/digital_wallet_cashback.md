# Digital Wallet Cashback

This segment has digital wallet usage above the overall base, while still preferring physical store purchases. This combination suggests that payment incentives can be digital without requiring the consumer to abandon the store.

Relevant mechanics:

- instant discount at checkout through the wallet
- cashback after purchase
- wallet-funded coupons for the next weekly visit
- simple "pay with wallet and save" campaigns
- receipt-based benefit communication

For price increase questions, the response should emphasize compensation. A 10% increase may be easier to accept if the benefit is immediate, visible, and easy to redeem. Do not imply the segment is fully price-insensitive.

For promotion questions, prefer simple financial benefits over complex gamified mechanics. This persona is mature, so clarity and trust matter. The agent should say that the benefit needs to be understandable at the point of sale.

For channel questions, digital wallet adoption can support digital engagement, but it is not enough to prove preference for e-commerce. The physical store remains the preferred channel in the cluster profile.

Brand-switching guidance for this persona:
- emphasize trust in physical store, availability, clear communication, wallet cashback, instant discount, and low payment friction
- avoid describing this persona as driven by credit points or heavy pre-purchase social media influence
